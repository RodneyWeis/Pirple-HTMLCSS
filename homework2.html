Nuts and Bolts cereal snack mix

This snack mix is always a favourite when friends gather. Whether it's a night of gaming, socializing or just getting together to watch the big game, everyone loves snacking on this crunchy treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Cheerios cereal
  • 2 cups Shreddies cereal
  • 2 cups pretzel sticks
  • 1 cup cheese nibs crackers
  • 1 cup Rice Chex cereal
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp onion salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large roasting pan 🥨
  3. Microwave the butter until melted 🧈
  4. Add Worcestershire sauce, onion salt and garlic powder to the butter; stir thoroughly 🧂
  5. Drizzle 2/3 of the butter mixture over the dry ingredients while stirring
  6. Cover roasting pan and bake for 30 minutes 🥘
  7. Drizzle remaining butter mixture over the dry ingredients while stirring
  8. Cover roasting pan and bake for a further 30 minutes
  9. Stir mixture and bake, uncovered, for 15 minutes
  10. Cool and serve 🥳

Visually, this dish is appealing with the golden-brown toasted cereal. The savoury smell from baking permeates the house, and accentuates the taste when you finally take that first crunchy bite. Enjoy!
